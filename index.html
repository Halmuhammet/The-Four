Urinalysis/Body Fluid Dashboard

This real-time dashboard is designed for the Urinalysis/Body Fluids department at a pathology lab. It supports LIS training for students and faculty, allowing faculty to create quality control files and students to work within defined parameters. The system includes sections for Quality Control, Patient Reports, Quizzes, and more.
